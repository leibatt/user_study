Welcome! We've created a visualization system called ScalaR to facilitate the exploration of scientific data. This website is part of a research study on the effectiveness of ScalaR as a visual data exploration tool.

  • Complete 2 questionnaires (10 minutes)

  • Learn how to use ScalaR (10 minutes)

  • Complete two research-related tasks using ScalaR (40 minutes)
