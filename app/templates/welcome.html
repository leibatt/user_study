Welcome! This website is part of a research study on the effectiveness of ScalaR, a visual data exploration tool.

If you are interested in participating in the study, click on the start button below. This will bring you to the consent page, which will describe the expectations and procedures of the study. After providing your consent to participate, you will be asked to do the following:

  • Complete 2 questionnaires (5 minutes)

  • Learn how to use ScalaR (5 minutes)

  • Complete 3 research-related tasks using ScalaR (25 minutes)

Participants will receive a $25 Amazon gift card for completing the study. More details on receiving the gift card will be provided upon completion of the user study.

Participation is entirely voluntary and you can stop the study at any time by closing the browser window/tab. No personally identifiable information will be requested or recorded in this user study.

Note: we are recording timing information for completing user study tasks. Please set aside the necessary time to complete each task without interruption, and please do not leave the website unattended for an extended period of time. Participation is automatically ended after 20 minutes of inactivity.
